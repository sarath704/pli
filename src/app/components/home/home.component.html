Pineland Prairie
Integrated Infrastructure Development

Pineland Prairie is Knight Kiplinger’s new greenfield city in Martin County, Florida, covering an area of nearly 3500 acres. Knight’s vision is to create a development that sets new standard for cities for the future. A city that is in harmony with nature, a city that nurtures its residents, a city that is vibrant, attractive and inclusive. Pineland Prairie’s plan and its vision has been acknowledged as the new standard for future projects by leading planning organizations. Local Planning Agency grants strong support for Pineland Prairie.

Pineland Infra is an infrastructure development agency, especially created to contribute to the vision of Knight Kiplinger by developing a truly integrated, secure & reliable infrastructure. Infrastructure is heart of any development, Pineland Infra aims to create an infrastructure that is sustainable at every level and truly democratic in how it is planned and how it will be operated.

The Circular Foundation – True Value Creation

As per UNEP estimates cities consume 75% of natural resources globally. They produce 50% of global waste and 60-80% of green house gas emissions. Cities are at the heart of the extractive, `take, make, dispose’ economy with their linear metabolism.

Pineland Infra will deliver an infrastructure that acknowledges finite resources, our biophysical limits and create closed loop systems in energy, water, waste & food production that are further in symbiosis with each other.

Infrastructure by design will be regenerative. Focus will be on end-use service delivery by converging investments, technology and spatial needs for all supply systems. Pinland Infra will set a precedence from current systems of energy, transport, digital information, water, waste and ﬂood protection that are built to provide unconstrained demand of end-use products, with larger consumption volumes corresponding to higher economic revenue.

Smart Infrastructure

Often named as – The Fourth Industrial Revolution, the exponential growth of digital connectivity and impending digital transformation – can create signiﬁcant new sources of value for citizens and economies. Pineland Prairie’s infrastructure will be designed with built-in intelligence such that all the supply chains, reuse and regeneration can be transformed into assets. This will lead to new business opportunities via performance models.

Driving Downstream Economy

Local physical infrastructure that is circular in nature along with the intelligence will create a breeding ground for innovation and value creation. Circular economy value drivers include extending the useful life and maximising the utilisation of assets, looping assets, and regenerating natural capital.

Internet of Things, Open Data, Connected Infrastructure will unleash to a broad range of opportunities and will lead to new local business opportunities via performance models, community services & citizen engagement.

Innovation – Creating the future

It is an established fact that de-centralised production or production closest to the use is the most efficient. New technologies today allow local production and regeneration, the potential for smart, integrated, decentralized infrastructure at Pineland Prairie is massive and Pineland Infra will explore possibilities to create highly sustainable and resilient subset of local microgrids for various supply systems from bottom-up.
